# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 5, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account data:** Email address, password (hashed), timezone, plan tier.
- **X connection data:** X user ID, username, OAuth tokens (encrypted at rest).
- **Post content:** Text and images you schedule for publication.
- **Usage data:** Post counts, scheduling activity, failure logs.
- **Payment data:** Processed by Stripe; we store Stripe customer ID only.
- **Technical data:** IP address, browser type (standard server logs).

## 2. How We Use Information

- To operate the scheduling service and publish posts on your behalf.
- To send transactional emails (e.g., publish failures).
- To process subscriptions and prevent abuse.
- To improve the Service and comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Third Parties

We share data with:

- **X (Twitter):** To publish posts via their API.
- **Stripe:** Payment processing.
- **Resend:** Transactional email delivery.
- **Hosting providers:** Vercel, Railway/Fly, database and Redis hosts.

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active.
- Scheduled post content is deleted when you delete posts or your account.
- OAuth tokens are deleted on disconnect or account deletion.

## 5. Security

OAuth tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Passwords are bcrypt-hashed. All traffic uses HTTPS.

## 6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data by contacting privacy@xqueue.app or deleting your account in Settings. EU/UK users may have additional rights under GDPR.

## 7. Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

## 8. Children

The Service is not intended for users under 13.

## 9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted on this page.
